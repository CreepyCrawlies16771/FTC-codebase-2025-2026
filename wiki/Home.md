## What is This?

The FTC Robot Controller is the Android application that runs on your robot's control hub. This wiki provides practical guides to help you:

- Understand how your robot system works
- Configure motors and servos properly
- Adjust PID controllers and movement parameters
- Get the best performance from your hardware
